# Pattern: Purchasing

**Outcome:** Controlled buy process with approvals and supplier performance.

## Common workflow

Need → RFQ/PO → approve → receive against PO → 3-way match (as required)

## Design principles

- Approval thresholds by amount/role
- PO ↔ receipt linkage
- Supplier scorecards over time
- Landed cost capture for imports

## Not allowed

Copying proprietary purchasing UIs or confidential price books.

## Mission

Transform business problems into production-ready software through a **governed, repeatable, AI-assisted delivery process**.

## Positioning (frozen)

**Jarvis is an AI Consulting Firm — not an AI software generator.**

> From business challenge to production software—guided by AI consulting, governed delivery, and a deterministic software factory.

> We don't just build software. We engineer better businesses.

Factory is **internal**. Clients experience a **consulting team**.

**Stage 4:** Business Decision Intelligence — every recommendation is explainable (why, sources, impact, cost, if excluded).

**Constitution:** [CONSTITUTION.md](./CONSTITUTION.md)  
**Product model:** [PRODUCT-MODEL.md](./PRODUCT-MODEL.md)  
**No more conceptual layers** — competitive advantage = execution with real clients.

## Jarvis interaction model

Clients describe **business**. Jarvis does **not** wait for a software brief.

Jarvis = Chief Business Consultant: interview (confidence ≥ 90%) → knowledge graph + industry intel → pattern benchmarks → gap analysis with **Decision Intelligence** → professional department review → executive package (maturity, ROI) → governance → internal factory.

Canonical: [JARVIS-CONSULTANT.md](./JARVIS-CONSULTANT.md)

## Competitive advantage

**Not** AI, coding agents, MCP, or prompts — everyone has those.

**Advantage is the closed loop:**

```
Client
  → Business Discovery
  → Business Intelligence
  → Commercial Intelligence
  → Governance
  → Deterministic Factory
  → Deployment
  → Support
  → Knowledge
  → Better Next Project
```

Consulting + delivery + continuous learning is hard to replicate. The Product Factory is **one stage**, not the product.

---

## Core principles

1. **Grabber Core stays frozen** except justified platform improvements (reliability, security, determinism, cost).
2. **Business workflows drive the factory**, not the other way around.
3. **Every project creates reusable knowledge.**
4. **Every reusable asset strengthens the Product Factory.**
5. **Every completed engagement improves future delivery.**
6. **Measure operational outcomes before adding features.**
